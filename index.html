Our mission is to grow Saskatchewan’s tech ecosystem not just by building impactful products, but by building skilled engineers too.

In parallel, I’m deeply involved in applied research and product development in embedded systems and medical electronics. I’m passionate about developing intelligent embedded solutions that bridge engineering and human impact — in healthcare, agriculture, and automation. I thrive at the intersection of innovation, execution, and mentorship.
